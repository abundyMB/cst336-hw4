Intro to the IoT

The last few years have seen smarter and more powerful technology enter our homes, places of work, and public spaces. As computing power increases, these devices are able to become smaller, more mobile, and more accessible.


The network of these devices is known as the Internet of Things (IoT), which combines smartphones with appliances, wearables, and other technologies. The IoT is able to share data, and from that, glean and utilize a great amount of information in order to enhance and assist in the lives of those who own such devices.


As of 2020, there are almost as many devices as there are people on the planet. This number will only continue to grow as the technology becomes cheaper and easier to acquire and integrate into the lives of those who use them.

"If you think that the internet has changed your life, think again. The IoT is about to change it all over again!"
- Brendan O'Brien, Chief Architect & Co-Founder, Aria Systems
