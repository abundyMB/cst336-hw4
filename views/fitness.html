Intro to Smart Fitness

A common trend that has taken hold over the last few years is the use of smart fitness devices, most often found in wearables, like FitBit or iWatch. These devices are used in conjunction with ye olde workouts of yore.


Other IoT devices allow for a more immersive workout. Whether this is through participation in fitness classes with smart bikes like Peloton, creating a leaderboard-like system where users can see each other's stats and compete against themselves and each other, or even utilizing virtual reality to allow runners on treadmills to jog through breathtaking vistas.

Fitness Device Examples:

Wearables
- FitBit
- iWatch
Class-Enabled
- Peloton
- Tempo Fitness
