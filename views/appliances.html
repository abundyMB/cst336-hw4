Intro to Smart Home Devices

Home smart devices are perhaps the most common of all the devices in the IoT. There is at least one, and likely dozens, of devices for every aspect of home living. These smart appliances can be controlled through Virtual Assistants or desktop/mobile apps.


Their functionality ranges from security to meal preparation to energy efficiency. There are devices, like Ring, that allow homeowners to see who is at their door. There are innumerable kitchen appliances that have been integrated with some smart functionality or another. Still more devices allow for control over electricity use, lighting, and temperature control.
